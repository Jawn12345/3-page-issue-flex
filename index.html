About Us

We are an organization focusing on the conservation of sea turtles around the world. We are based in the United States. In the past 50 years, we have work with educational institutions to promote awareness of marine ecosytems. We study sea turtles across the world to better understand their biology and come up with innovated techniques to better protect these endangered species. Donations would greatly help support our cause.

Our team is passionate about sea turtles. We hold bi-weekly beach cleanups to not only keep our beaches clean, but also to protect our marine ecosytem.
